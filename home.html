Pups Parlor

Big and small, young and old, at Pups Parlor all dogs are groomed with patience and precision. Every pup should enjoy their visit to the groomers!

Is incredibly important to keep your canine clean for not only their physical health, but their mental health as well. The cleaner the pup, the happier they will be. We offer quality assurance with our experienced and dedicated dog groomers. We want to ensure your canine remains calm and is not under too much stress during their time here. Although our services are extensive, we tailor each dog to their specific needs. Learn More about our services.
